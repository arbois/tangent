I wanted to make sure you know about a programme I think could use your help finding the right candidates.

Tangent is a 24-month programme providing up to S$200,000 per participant for young people in Singapore who want to build something on their own terms. The right candidate is probably not your top academic student — more likely the one with an idea they keep talking about, or the one who is motivated and self-directed but restless within the conventional system.

It starts with a four-week programme: S$1,000, AI tools, and human guides. No mandatory courses, no assignments. Participants define the work and show results at the end of four weeks. Several universities have already agreed to defer matriculation for participants. Age range is 18–21 for women and men without NS obligations, and 18–22 for men with NS obligations.

If you know someone who belongs in Tangent, please refer them. And if you know other teachers or referrers who should know about this, please pass it on.

https://tangent.org.sg/ has details on the programme and how it works, how to apply, how to refer someone, and how to get involved as a guide.
